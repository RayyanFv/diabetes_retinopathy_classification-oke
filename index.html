What is Diabetic Retinopathy? Learn more about it.

Diabetic retinopathy is a diabetes complication that affects eyes. It's caused by damage to the blood vessels of the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye (retina).

How We Help. Our solution.

Using state-of-the-art deep learning models, RetinoCapture helps detect diabetic retinopathy with high accuracy, enabling early intervention and better management of the condition.
